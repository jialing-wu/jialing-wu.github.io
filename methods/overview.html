What Research Problem Are You Facing?

按研究场景组织的方法指南。找到你的问题，点进对应课程。

Method guides organized by research scenario. Find your problem, click the course.

I want to estimate X's effect on Y
我想估计 X 对 Y 的效应
▶
Regression Analysis
回归分析
X predicts Y by how much? OLS basics, multiple regression, diagnostics, and interaction effects.
X 能预测多少 Y？OLS 基础、多元回归、诊断和交互效应。
→
▶
Causal Inference
因果推断
Beyond correlation: RCT, DiD, IV, RDD, synthetic control, and heterogeneous treatment effects.
超越相关：RCT、双重差分、工具变量、断点回归、合成控制和异质性处理效应。
→
▶
Empirical Modeling
实证建模
Interaction terms, spatial dependence, simultaneity (2SLS), and dynamic models (VAR, ECM).
交互项、空间依赖、联立方程（2SLS）和动态模型（VAR、ECM）。
→
My data has a special structure
我的数据有特殊结构
▶
Multilevel Models
多层模型
Students in schools, voters in districts — hierarchical/nested data with random intercepts and slopes.
学生嵌套在学校、选民嵌套在选区——层级数据的随机截距和随机斜率。
→
▶
Time Series & Panel Data
时间序列与面板数据
Repeated observations over time: stationarity, cointegration, fixed/random effects, and event studies.
跨时间的重复观测：平稳性、协整、固定/随机效应和事件研究。
→
▶
Network Analysis
网络分析
Nodes and edges: centrality, community detection, diffusion, and contagion on social networks.
节点与连边：中心性、社区检测、扩散与社会网络上的传染。
→
I want to predict or handle high-dimensional data
我想做预测或处理高维数据
▶
Machine Learning
机器学习
Prediction vs. explanation: regularization (LASSO/Ridge), trees, random forests, and interpretability.
预测 vs. 解释：正则化（LASSO/Ridge）、决策树、随机森林和可解释性。
→
▶
Text as Data
文本分析
Turn documents into numbers: preprocessing, TF-IDF, topic models, embeddings, and LLM annotation.
把文本变成数字：预处理、TF-IDF、主题模型、词嵌入和大语言模型标注。
→
I want to validate my measurement instrument
我想确认我的测量工具是否可靠
▶
Measurement & Scaling
测量与量表
Scale development, reliability (α, ω), EFA/CFA, and Item Response Theory (IRT).
量表开发、信度（α、ω）、探索性/验证性因子分析和项目反应理论（IRT）。
→
▶
Structural Equation Models
结构方程模型
Model entire systems: path analysis, latent variables, CFA + structural paths, and fit indices.
对整个系统建模：路径分析、潜变量、CFA + 结构路径和拟合指标。
→
I'm not sure my model is reliable
我不确定我的模型是否靠谱
▶
Evaluating Model Robustness
模型稳健性评估
Functional form, omitted variables, measurement error, specification curves, and sensitivity analysis.
函数形式、遗漏变量、测量误差、设定曲线和敏感性分析。
→
▶
MLE & Generalized Linear Models
最大似然与广义线性模型
When OLS doesn't fit: logit, probit, Poisson, and the logic of maximum likelihood estimation.
当 OLS 不够用时：logit、probit、Poisson 和最大似然估计的逻辑。
→
▶
Bayesian Modeling
贝叶斯建模
Priors, posteriors, MCMC: when you want to encode prior knowledge or get full uncertainty distributions.
先验、后验、MCMC：当你想融入先验知识或获得完整的不确定性分布时。
→
I want to understand how social systems work
我想理解社会系统的运作逻辑
▶
Theoretical Modeling
理论建模
Turn verbal theories into equations/simulations: game theory, ABM, network diffusion, Schelling segregation.
把口头理论变成方程/模拟：博弈论、ABM、网络扩散、Schelling 隔离。
→
▶
Rational Choice Theory
理性选择理论
Expected utility, game theory, collective action problems, and behavioral extensions (Prospect Theory).
期望效用、博弈论、集体行动困境和行为扩展（前景理论）。
→

种族、民族与量化方法
