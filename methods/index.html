Research Methods Notebook

Connecting method to question — one paper at a time.

Despite decades of statistical training in graduate programs, one frustration persists: you have a research question, but you don't know which method fits. Courses tend to either skim the surface or go deep on just one approach — rarely giving you a broad map of the terrain. These AI-assisted notes aim to fill that gap, providing a brief overview of each method paired with a real paper walkthrough. Topics draw partly on the ICPSR Summer Program and SICSS.
