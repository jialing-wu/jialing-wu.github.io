研究方法笔记本

Research Methods Notebook

研究生阶段最常见的困境之一：遇到研究问题，不知道该用什么方法。统计课程要么浅尝辄止，要么只深入某一个方向——很难建立起横跨各类方法的全局视野。这个 AI 协助整理的笔记希望能填补这个空白，为每种方法提供简要概述并搭配真实论文案例拆解。学习主题部分来自 ICPSR Summer Program 和计算社会科学暑期学院（SICSS）。

One of the common frustrations in grad school: you have a research question but don't know which method fits. Statistics courses tend to either skim the surface or go deep on just one approach — rarely giving you a broad map. These AI-assisted notes aim to fill that gap, providing a brief overview of each method paired with a real paper walkthrough. Topics and methods draw partly on the ICPSR Summer Program and the Summer Institute in Computational Social Science (SICSS).

Jialing Wu  ·  Ph.D. Student, The Ohio State University  ·  Feb 2026
