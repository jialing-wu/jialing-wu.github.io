Latent Profile Analysis

潜在剖面分析

Sample · 15–20 min
What Is This?
这是什么？

Latent Profile Analysis (LPA) is a person-centered method that identifies hidden subgroups in your data. Instead of assuming everyone comes from one population, LPA discovers distinct profiles — groups of people who share similar patterns of characteristics. This is essential for understanding heterogeneity in psychology, education, and social science.

潜在剖面分析（LPA）是一种以个体为中心的方法，可识别数据中的隐藏亚组。不是假设样本中的每个人都来自同一个总体，而是 LPA 发现不同的剖面——具有相似特征模式的人群。这对心理学、教育和社会科学中理解异质性至关重要。

Audience
受众

Graduate students across disciplines. Basic understanding of means, standard deviations, and regression is helpful but not required. We build from simple clustering to model-based classification step by step. No prior LPA experience assumed.

跨学科研究生。对均值、标准差和回归的基本理解会有帮助但非必需。我们从简单聚类逐步建立到基于模型的分类。无需先前的 LPA 经验。

How LPA Works: Mixture Models

LPA 的工作原理：混合模型

LPA is built on a Finite Mixture Model: observed data is a mixture of K normal distributions, each representing a hidden subgroup. Using the EM algorithm, LPA infers how many subgroups exist, the mean and variance of each on each indicator, and each person's probability of belonging to each subgroup.

LPA 建立在有限混合模型之上：观测数据是 K 个正态分布的混合，每个分布代表一个隐藏的亚组。通过 EM 算法，LPA 推断出有多少个亚组、每个亚组在每个指标上的均值和方差，以及每个个体属于各亚组的概率。

Overlapping Gaussians
重叠的正态分布
Three profiles (red, gold, tea) overlap. Each person's scores are drawn from one of these distributions.
三个剖面（红、金、茶色）重叠。每个人的得分来自这些分布中的一个。

Probabilistic Classification: Each individual has a probability of belonging to each class — not hard assignment. Classification uses the highest posterior probability. Example: a student may have posterior probabilities of .82, .13, and .05 for Profiles 1, 2, and 3 — they would be assigned to Profile 1.

概率性分类：每个个体都有属于每个类别的概率——不是硬性分配。最终分类取后验概率最高的类别。例如：某学生属于 Profile 1、2、3 的后验概率分别为 .82、.13、.05 → 分配到 Profile 1。

Key insight: Unlike hard clustering, LPA quantifies uncertainty. You assign to the highest probability class but acknowledge 18% chance this person belongs elsewhere.
关键洞见：与硬聚类不同，LPA 量化了不确定性。你将其分配到概率最高的类别，但承认这个人属于其他类别的 18% 概率。

Choosing the Right Number of Profiles

选择合适的剖面数量

BIC
BIC
Bayesian Information Criterion
贝叶斯信息准则
Lower values indicate better fit. Look for the "elbow" where the decrease slows down. Penalizes model complexity.
较低的值表示拟合更好。寻找下降趋缓的"拐点"。惩罚模型复杂度。
BLRT
BLRT
Bootstrapped Likelihood Ratio Test
自助似然比检验
Most accurate test across conditions. Significant p-value (< .05) means k classes fit better than k-1. Keep adding classes until p ≥ .05.
在各种条件下最准确的检验。p < .05 表示 k 个类别优于 k-1 个。继续增加直到 p ≥ .05。
Entropy
Entropy
Classification Quality
分类质量
Ranges 0–1. Values > .80 indicate clear class separation. But DO NOT use it for class selection alone.
范围 0–1。> .80 表示清晰的类别分离。但不要仅用它来选择类别数。

Decision Rule: When indices disagree, prioritize BLRT + BIC, combined with theoretical interpretability. Class sizes should be at least 5–8% of your sample to avoid unstable profiles.

决策规则：指标不一致时，优先考虑 BLRT + BIC，结合理论可解释性。类别大小应该至少是样本的 5–8% 以避免不稳定的剖面。

Should You Use LPA?

你应该使用 LPA 吗？

01
Good for LPA
适合用 LPA
You suspect distinct subgroups with different patterns. You care about who belongs to which group. You have continuous indicators measured on similar scales. Your research question focuses on heterogeneity.
你怀疑存在具有不同模式的不同亚组。你关心谁属于哪个组。你有在相似量表上测量的连续型指标。你的研究问题关注异质性。
02
Caution
谨慎
Small sample sizes (N < 300). Categorical or mixed indicators — use LCA instead. Imbalanced class sizes may create unstable profiles. Always validate with theoretical and external criteria.
样本量小（N < 300）。分类或混合型指标——改用 LCA。不平衡的类别大小可能创建不稳定的剖面。始终用理论和外部标准验证。
03
Not LPA
不适合 LPA
Your question is about variable relationships (use regression). You expect one normal population. You only have time-series data without cross-sectional variation. You want dimension reduction (use PCA/factor analysis).
你的问题是关于变量关系（改用回归）。你期望一个正态总体。你只有时间序列数据而没有横截面差异。你想要降维（改用 PCA/因子分析）。

Paper Walkthrough: Motivation Profiles in Math

论文拆解：数学动机剖面

Citation: Wang, C. K. J., Liu, W. C., Nie, Y., Chye, S., Lim, B. S. C., Liem, G. A., Tay, E. G., Hong, Y.-Y., & Chiu, C.-Y. (2017). Latent profile analysis of students' motivation and outcomes in mathematics: An organismic integration theory perspective. Heliyon, 3(6), e00308.

论文：Wang, C. K. J., Liu, W. C., Nie, Y., Chye, S., Lim, B. S. C., Liem, G. A., Tay, E. G., Hong, Y.-Y., & Chiu, C.-Y. (2017). 学生数学动机和成果的潜在剖面分析：有机整合理论视角。Heliyon, 3(6), e00308。

Core question: Are there distinct subgroups of students characterized by unique combinations of motivation types from Self-Determination Theory? Do these profiles predict different academic outcomes (effort, competence, task value, study time)?

核心问题：学生中是否存在具有自我决定理论中独特动机组合特征的不同亚组？这些剖面是否能预测不同的学业结果（努力、胜任感、任务价值、学习时间）？

Research Design & Indicator Selection

研究设计与指标选择

The study used Self-Regulation Questionnaire–Academic (SRQ-A) with four LPA indicators (7-point Likert scale): External Regulation (4 items), Introjected Regulation (4 items), Identified Regulation (3 items), and Intrinsic Motivation (3 items). Sample: N = 1,151 secondary students (age 13–17, M = 14.69) from 5 schools in Singapore.

研究采用学业自我调节问卷（SRQ-A），包含四个 LPA 指标（7 点 Likert 量表）：外部调节（4 题）、内摄调节（4 题）、认同调节（3 题）和内在动机（3 题）。样本：来自新加坡 5 所学校的 N = 1,151 名中学生（年龄 13–17 岁，M = 14.69）。

Why these four? Self-Determination Theory posits a continuum of self-determination: external → introjected → identified → intrinsic. A person-centered lens reveals that not everyone falls at one point; people cluster into profiles based on combinations of these four.

为什么选这四个？自我决定理论假设自我决定的连续体：外部 → 内摄 → 认同 → 内在。以个体为中心的视角揭示，并非每个人都处于一个点；人们根据这四个的组合聚集成剖面。

Estimation: Mplus 7.2 with MLR estimator, 10,000 random starts (500 best solutions retained). Models with 1–8 profiles were estimated and compared.

估计方法：Mplus 7.2 with MLR 估计器，10,000 个随机起始值（保留 500 个最佳解）。估计并比较了 1–8 个剖面的模型。

Step 1: Model Comparison & Class Enumeration

步骤一：模型比较与类别数确定

Result: The 4-profile solution was selected. The aLMR p-value became non-significant (p = .12) beyond 4 profiles, indicating that adding a 5th profile did not significantly improve the model. BIC showed a clear elbow at k=4, and each of the four profiles was theoretically meaningful and interpretable. All indices converged on the same answer.

结果：选择了 4 剖面方案。超过 4 个剖面后 aLMR p 值变得不显著（p = .12），表明添加第 5 个剖面未能显著改进模型。BIC 在 k=4 处出现清晰的拐点，四个剖面都理论上有意义且可解释。所有指标都一致指向同一答案。

i.e.: After fitting models with 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 profiles, the researchers examined BIC (lower is better), BLRT (p < .05 means add another profile), and entropy (> .80 is good). At k=4, BIC stopped improving much, BLRT became non-significant, and each profile had a distinct meaning. This is the sweet spot.

即：拟合 1、2、3、4、5、6、7、8 个剖面的模型后，研究人员检查了 BIC（越低越好）、BLRT（p < .05 说明增加剖面数）和 entropy（> .80 为好）。在 k=4 时，BIC 改善幅度不大，BLRT 变得不显著，每个剖面都有独特含义。这是最优点。

Step 3: Outcome Validation — Finding 1

步骤三：结果验证—发现 1

Result: The Autonomous profile (P3) consistently outperformed all other groups across every outcome: effort (3 > 2 > 4 > 1), task value (3 > 2 = 4 > 1), perceived competence (3 > 4 > 2 = 1), and math study hours (3 > 4 = 2 = 1).

结果：自主型（P3）在所有结果变量上均优于其他组：努力（3 > 2 > 4 > 1）、任务价值（3 > 2 = 4 > 1）、感知胜任力（3 > 4 > 2 = 1）和数学学习时间（3 > 4 = 2 = 1）。

i.e.: Students with high autonomous motivation (Identified + Intrinsic) spent more time on math, reported more effort, felt more competent, and valued the subject more. This is not just correlation — these are distinct profile groups showing different behavioral and psychological patterns.

即：具有高自主动机（认同+内在）的学生花在数学上的时间更多，报告了更多努力，感到更能胜任，也更重视该科目。这不只是相关性——这些是不同的剖面群体，显示不同的行为和心理模式。

Step 3: Outcome Validation — Finding 2

步骤三：结果验证—发现 2

Result: Effort showed a clear gradient across profiles: P3 > P2 > P4 > P1. Notably, the Externally Driven group (P2) reported higher effort than the Moderate group (P4), suggesting external pressure can sustain effort — but the Autonomous group's effort still surpassed all others.

结果：努力在各剖面间呈现清晰的梯度：P3 > P2 > P4 > P1。值得注意的是，外部驱动组（P2）的努力高于中等组（P4），说明外部压力能维持努力——但自主组的努力仍然高于所有其他组。

i.e.: External pressure (high external regulation) can make students work harder — P2 demonstrates this. But intrinsic interest is more powerful. The Autonomous profile's effort wasn't forced; it was driven by genuine interest in math. This distinction matters for educational policy.

即：外部压力（高外部调节）可以让学生更努力工作——P2 展示了这一点。但内在兴趣更强大。自主剖面的努力不是被迫的，而是由对数学的真正兴趣驱动。这个区别对教育政策很重要。

Step 3: Outcome Validation — Finding 3

步骤三：结果验证—发现 3

Result: The Externally Driven profile (P2) showed no advantage in perceived competence over the Low Motivation group (P1), despite P2's higher external and identified regulation (2 = 1). In contrast, even the Moderate group (P4) outperformed P2 in competence, suggesting that intrinsic interest — not external pressure — is essential for building academic confidence.

结果：外部驱动型（P2）在感知胜任力上相比低动机组（P1）没有任何优势，尽管 P2 的外部调节和认同调节更高（2 = 1）。相反，中等组（P4）在胜任感上优于 P2，说明内在兴趣——而非外部压力——是建立学业自信的关键。

i.e.: You can coerce effort through external demands, but you cannot coerce confidence. Competence comes from genuine engagement, not compliance. This is a critical insight: policies that rely only on external pressure (grades, tests) may boost effort but won't build self-belief.

即：你可以通过外部要求强制努力，但不能强制自信。胜任感来自真正的投入，而非顺从。这是一个关键洞见：仅依赖外部压力（成绩、考试）的政策可能会促进努力，但不会建立自我信念。

Extensions: From Cross-Sectional to Longitudinal

拓展：从横截面到纵向

Cross-Group Comparison
跨组比较

Morin et al. (2016) Six-Step Framework: Test similarity of profiles across groups (e.g., countries, genders).

1. Configural (same # of profiles?)
2. Structural (same means?)
3. Dispersion (same variances?)
4. Distributional (same proportions?)
5. Predictive (same predictors?)
6. Explanatory (same outcomes?)

Morin et al. (2016) 六步框架：测试跨组剖面的相似性（如国家、性别）。

1. 形态（剖面数相同？）
2. 结构（均值相同？）
3. 离散（方差相同？）
4. 分布（比例相同？）
5. 预测（预测因素相同？）
6. 解释（结果相同？）

Longitudinal Extensions
纵向拓展

Latent Transition Analysis (LTA): Track profile transitions over time. How many students move from Low → Autonomous?

Growth Mixture Modeling (GMM): Identify trajectory classes (stable, improving, declining).

Other: Multilevel LPA, factor mixture models, Bayesian estimation.

潜在转变分析（LTA）：追踪随时间的剖面转变。有多少学生从低动机 → 自主型转变？

增长混合模型（GMM）：识别轨迹类别（稳定、改善、下降）。

其他：多层次 LPA、因子混合模型、贝叶斯估计。

LPA is the foundation — expand it with time, groups, and complexity.
LPA 是基础——用时间、群体和复杂性扩展它。
