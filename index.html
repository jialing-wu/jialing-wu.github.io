研究方法笔记本

Research Methods Notebook

研究生阶段最常见的困境之一：遇到研究问题，不知道该用什么方法。学校的课程要么浅尝辄止，要么只深入某一个方向——很难建立起横跨统计与计算社会科学的方法视野。这个 AI 协助整理的笔记，希望能提供一个初步的参考框架。学习主题部分来自 ICPSR Summer Program 和计算社会科学暑期学院（SICSS）。

每个方法大类包含两个部分：基础（Foundation）以「遇到什么情况、应该用什么方法」为导向，介绍各类方法的适用场景和核心概念；运用（Sample）提供方法简介，并搭配真实论文案例，看研究者怎么实际用这些方法。

One of the most common frustrations in grad school: you have a research question but don't know which method fits. Courses tend to either skim the surface or go deep on just one approach — rarely giving you a broad map. These AI-assisted notes aim to fill that gap, drawing partly on ICPSR Summer Program and the Summer Institute in Computational Social Science (SICSS).

Each method category has two parts: Foundation — oriented around "what situation calls for this method"; and Sample — a brief overview paired with real paper examples.

Jialing Wu  ·  Ph.D. Student, The Ohio State University  ·  Feb 2026
