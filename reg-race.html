Race, Ethnicity & Quantitative Methodology

Methods · Specialized 16
What Is This?
这一页讲什么？

Race and ethnicity are among the most consequential variables in social science — and among the most misused. This page addresses how race and ethnicity are defined, measured, and analyzed in quantitative research, and how researchers can avoid common methodological pitfalls.

种族和族裔是社会科学中最重要、也最常被误用的变量。本页探讨量化研究中种族和族裔是如何定义、测量和分析的，以及研究者如何避免常见的方法论陷阱。
