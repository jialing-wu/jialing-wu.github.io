Data Science & Text Analysis

Computational Methods · Advanced 14
What Is This?
这一页讲什么？

Speeches, manifestos, social media posts, court opinions, news articles — political and social life produces vast amounts of text. Text-as-data methods let you turn unstructured text into quantitative measures suitable for statistical analysis.

演讲、宣言、社交媒体帖子、法院意见、新闻报道——政治和社会生活产生了大量文本。文本数据方法让你将非结构化文本转化为适合统计分析的量化指标。
