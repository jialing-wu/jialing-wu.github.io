Rational Choice Theories of Politics and Society

Theory · Specialized 17
What Is This?
这一页讲什么？

Why do people vote when the probability of being decisive is essentially zero? Why do countries cooperate sometimes and defect other times? Rational choice theory provides a unified framework for thinking about strategic behavior — and for understanding when and why it fails.

为什么人们在几乎不可能成为决定性一票的情况下还要投票？为什么国家有时合作、有时背叛？理性选择理论提供了思考策略性行为的统一框架——以及理解它何时、为何失效。
